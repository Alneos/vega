Parse and build an Orientation referentiel.

P1 is the origin O P1P2 make the Ox axis We then parse either vectorr V or a point P3 (v=P1P3) which defines Oy and Oz. See, for example, the help on the CBAR keyword (page 1154 of MDN Nastran 2006 Quick Reference Guide.)
